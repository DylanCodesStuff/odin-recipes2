Gooey Gooey Mac N' Cheese

Description

This cheesy, gooey, delicious Mac N' Cheese is going to blow you away.

I can't wait for you to experience this punch of flavor!

Ingredients

  • Elbow Macaroni
  • Kosher Salt
  • Evaporated Milk
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • Franks Red Hot Sauce
  • Ground Mustard
  • Sharp Cheddar, Grated
  • American Cheese, Cubed
  • Cornstarch
  • Unsalted Butter

  1. Place macaroni in saucepan
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, hot sauce, and mustard.
  3. Drain pasta and return to saucepan.
  4. Combine and serve with bread crumbs if desired.
