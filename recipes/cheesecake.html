Super Sweet Cheesecake

Description

This sweet, sensational, and spectacular cheesecake is going to blow you away.

I can't wait for you to experience this punch of flavor!

Ingredients

  • Graham Cracker Crumbs
  • Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Butter
  • Cream Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Salt
  • Eggs

  1. Preheat Oven to 325F
  2. Combine Graham Cracker Crumbs with sugar, brown sugar, and melted butter.
  3. Mix cream cheese and sugar + sour cream, vanilla extract and salt.
  4. Throw your mixer at the wall and go buy a cheesecake factory frozen cake.
